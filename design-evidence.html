Design Evidence

Colour Theory

I chose a picture of a starry sky as my header photo for the website since I am myself interested in space and the universe and the blue sky gives off a calming but yet intriguing feeling. I decided that a white colour for the text on the header would make the letters pop out and not blend in to the header photo as maybe a darker colour would. As you scroll down the page, a black navigation bar forms behind the letters to ensure that the links are still visible as you navigate the page and is also a nice contrast from all the bright colours. While hovering over the navigation bar links you can see that they turn a light blue which creates a nice blend with the sky themed header. I used HEX codes to ensure that I found the exact colour for the texts. According to the Principles of Beautiful Web Design, specific colours can evoke a specific feeling in your brain. It is called colour psychology. The colour blue gives off a calming serene feeling. It also symbolizes openness and intelligence which I believe to be my most charming attributes.

Typography

When choosing my fonts and typography I wanted to choose fonts that were easy to read yet elegant. The only eye catching font is the one I chose for my name which is also a link back to the homepage. I wanted my font choice to say something about my personality. The fonts I chose were, Cinzel (for headers), Roboto Slab (for the page content) and Henny Penny (for my name on the navigation bar). Roboto Slab and Cinzel are both from the Serif font family. Cinzel is a modern serif font that blends in on any page. Roboto is a slab style font which is eye-catching and is more suitable for headlines. According to Jason Beaird James George, & Alex Walker who wrote “The Principles of Beautiful Web Design”, slab serifs were created to be readable from a distance, they make great headlines for advertising and have had a big increase in popularity on the Internet. In order to have a visually pleasing website the typography used must go with the theme of the site. I took advantage of the great website Google Fonts to ensure the fonts I chose were the best fit for my website. The fonts are bold and scaled to perfection so every word sticks off the page and is readable regardless of any eye sight issues.
